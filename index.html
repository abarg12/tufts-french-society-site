Apprendre en parlant

Tufts French Society is a student-run organization created as a space for French language learners and anyone who is interested in learning more about francophone culture.

We run a conversation circle called French Table every other week. There will also be events throughout the semester and other opportunities to get involved. Check the calendar for specific dates and times.
